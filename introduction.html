Introduction

Apart from major- and minor-specific coursework, I enjoy taking electives in music, journalism, physics, and philosophy. At the university, I work at a lab called "Beyond Categories", where I contribute to several projects in cognitive neuroscience, and lead one of them, which broadly falls under psycholinguistics.

I'm interested in math, computer science, linguistics, philosophy, the brain, music, and complex systems of all kinds (in no particular order).
I'm a free/open-source software (FOSS) enthusiast. I personally try to stick exclusively to open-source software, making exceptions only if I have to.

In my free time, I like to listen to music of many kinds and sing classical music, play football (soccer), take pictures of the surroundings, typeset documents in LaTeX, build NLP neural networks, and re-read dialogues from "Gödel, Escher, Bach: An eternal golden braid".
